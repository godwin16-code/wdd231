About Our Restaurant

Celebrating local agriculture and sustainable dining

Our Story

Harvest & Hearth was founded on a simple belief: that the best dining experiences start with the best ingredients. Located in the heart of Idaho Falls, our restaurant celebrates the rich agricultural heritage of the region and the passionate farmers who make it possible.

Our chef brings decades of culinary experience and a deep commitment to sustainability and local sourcing. Every dish on our menu tells the story of partnership between our kitchen and the family farms that surround us.

We invite you to experience what farm-to-table dining truly means: fresh, flavorful, and deeply connected to the land and community that nourish us.

Our Philosophy

Local Sourcing

We work exclusively with local farms and purveyors within a 150-mile radius of our restaurant. This commitment ensures freshness while supporting the local economy.

Seasonal Menus

Our menu changes with the seasons to feature what's at peak freshness. This approach aligns with natural agricultural cycles and delivers superior flavor.

Sustainability

We're committed to minimizing our environmental impact through responsible sourcing, waste reduction, and support for organic and regenerative farming practices.
