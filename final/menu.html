About Our Menu

At Harvest & Hearth, we believe that great food starts with great ingredients. Our menu changes seasonally to reflect what's freshly available from our local farm partners. Each dish is carefully crafted by our chef to showcase the natural flavors and quality of locally-sourced ingredients.

We work closely with family farms and local producers throughout Idaho to bring you the freshest, most flavorful dining experience possible. From heirloom vegetables to grass-fed meats, every ingredient tells a story of local agriculture and stewardship.
